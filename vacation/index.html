What is Budget Vacations?

Our History

Budget Vacations was founded in 2006 by Leigh Keightley, who grew frustrated when planning a trip with his family to England in the summer of 2006. Being unsatisfied by other options available, Leigh opted to found his own travel agency with his wife, with a dedication to simple plans, low costs and friendly employees.
